Events

Vivante is coming at you in full swing this year, with a whole host of fun filled events. Take a look at what we have planed in store for you.

From pages to the stage: Watch as the team at Jijivisha, IISER Tirupati's very own Drama Club bring to live a british classic, "The Monkey's Paw" by WW Jacobs. Unlike the main character of the play, we're sure you won't have any regrets in attending this extravaganza

Vivante's music competition enlivened this year by our talented students turning the ordinary number into the extraordinary! Being good will just not do! You've got to infuse every note with your inner fire fuelled by practise and persistence! Be it a solo or gorup performance, you gotta bring your A-game. Compete against fellow musicians, challenge your limits, and let your talent soar. The spotlight awaits you! So sign up to showcase your inner talent and take your chance to shine onstage!!

Dive into the heart of our carnival and experience the magic of dance like never before. Let your inhibitions melt away and your spirits soar high as you join us in this spectacular extravaganza of rhythm and movement. Show off your passion, flair, and boundless joy on our stage. Let the music move you and the audience be captivated by your performance.

Experience the magic of mime at Vivante'24, our annual college fest! Join the First Ever Mime Competition in VIVANTE history. Showcase your creativity and expression through silence and gesture. Teams of 5-7 members, 5-minute duration, no dialogues or props. Enrich the vibrant atmosphere and captivate the audience! Register now!
